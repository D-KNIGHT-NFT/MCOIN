Reality shifted. An obscured crossroad. Banishment undefined. A catalyst to break through, it is to trust your deep intuition.
This space, serves for me as a sanctuary, a mausoleo of memories, learnings, deceives and determination. The works presented, were born 2 years ago. Bringing this project to fruition, it is a very much needed release with full intentional motifs
Many of the artwork, are extracts of an inner journey of ancient wisdom and connected minds. The protagonists for this quest: A lone Wolf, a curious child and the creative motor, source of all answers. I hope you enjoy the oniric environment intended for both, creation and recreation.
As a WIP and "living" space, imperfect but transparent, feedback or comments are highly appreciated.
