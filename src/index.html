LIMBUS

Limbusfrom Latin, means "edge","boundary", as well as Limbus corneae, which describes the transition zone between the horn (cornea) and sclera of the eyeball. In Catholic theology refers to a place where souls are or were excluded from heaven and the eternal vision of God through no fault of their own.
